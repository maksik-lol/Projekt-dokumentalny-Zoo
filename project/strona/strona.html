PROJECT: PUBLIC ZOO

This project explores the quiet reality behind a “free” zoo — a place open to everyone, yet closed to those who live inside it. Nothing here looks overtly wrong, yet something feels unsettled. Small details — a glance, a condition, a moment — raise questions about how these animals truly live. In a space where rules exist but are not always followed, it reminds us that we may never fully understand what life feels like from their side.
